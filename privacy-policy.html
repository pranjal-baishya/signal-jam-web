Signal Jam — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Signal Jam is a puzzle game developed and published by Pranjal Dev Studio. We value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: If you create an account (email/password or Google Sign-In), we store your email address, display name, and a unique user ID. This is used to sync your progress across devices and for leaderboard display.

Game progress: Level completions, stars, coins, achievements, XP, streaks, power-ups, and settings. Stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our cloud servers (Google Firebase Firestore) if you are signed in.

Leaderboard data: Your display name, total stars, highest level, and player title are stored on our servers to power global leaderboards.

Crash reports: Anonymous crash data (device model, OS version, stack trace) is sent to Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. No personal information is included.

Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns (levels played, session duration, feature usage). This data is anonymous and aggregated.

Advertising: We may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or files
  • No microphone or camera access
  • No health or financial data

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To display leaderboards and player rankings
  • To fix crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand usage patterns and improve the game
  • To display relevant advertisements

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics) — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (Advertising) — Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (Authentication) — Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Your game progress is stored as long as your account exists. You can delete your data at any time by using the "Reset All Progress" option in the app settings, or by contacting us to request full account deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Signal Jam is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. The game may contain ads served by Google AdMob that comply with Google's child-directed content policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (visible in-app via your profile)
  • Delete your data (via "Reset All Progress" or by contacting us)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (via device settings)
  • Request account deletion by emailing us

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Google Firebase to protect your data. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
